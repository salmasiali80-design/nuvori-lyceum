Why This Division Exists

You already know English. It's gone quiet, not gone.

NUVORI LYCEUM is a maintenance, recovery and activation programme for adults who learned English properly once and want to keep using it as a living tool — not a subject to finish.

Each session pairs one credible source with one realistic case that has no clean answer. You prepare, you take a position, you defend it under challenge, and you leave having actually used the language, not just reviewed it.

Not a beginner course Not level-completion Not exam preparation
The Route

Every session, the same five moves.
